As the saying goes, the best things come in small packages, and nowhere is this more true than Monaco. But herein lies the challenge: breaking through the barriers of what people think they know about one of the most glamourous, and simultaneously smallest, countries in the world.The aim was to make the Principality closer to general public by providing digital access to all its monuments, rich history, and major events.

Stategy & Approach Monaco hardly needs an introduction, as most people know it as famous for “something” - whether that be its illustrious royal family, decadent casinos, or breathtaking beaches.But our goal was to move beyond the surface and take users on a storytelling journey around the places and events that truly make Monaco a dream country both to live in and visit.

Final Experience The final result combined sleek and intuitive navigation with refined design on all devices. MyMonaco offers unique, mulitlayer way of exploring and discovering the Principality.
